# Visitor Guide: Talking with Barbara's Digital Twin

Welcome! This guide will help you have better conversations with Barbara's digital twin at [twin.barbhs.com](https://twin.barbhs.com).

## What is this?

This is a conversational AI that embodies Barbara Hidalgo-Sotelo's professional knowledge, project portfolio, and personal expertise. It uses RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to retrieve relevant context from Barbara's curated knowledge base and respond in her voice.

**Think of it as:** An intelligent interface to explore Barbara's background, projects, and thinking — available 24/7 to answer questions a human Barbara would need hours to compile.

## Tips for Better Conversations

### ✅ Do

- **Ask follow-up questions** — The twin maintains conversation context
- **Request specifics** — "Walk me through the Resume Explorer architecture"
- **Explore connections** — "How does your dissertation relate to your current work?"
- **Ask for examples** — "Show me a project that demonstrates X skill"
- **Go deep on projects** — Many projects have walkthrough modes with diagrams

### ❌ Don't

- **Ask for real-time information** — The knowledge base has a cutoff date
- **Expect live website interaction** — The twin can describe but not browse sites
- **Request proprietary details** — Only publicly shared information is available
- **Assume omniscience** — If something isn't in the knowledge base, the twin will say so

## Understanding Responses

### Source-Based Answers

The twin's responses are grounded in Barbara's actual knowledge base:
- Biographical sketch (authoritative source of truth)
- Philosophy and professional positioning documents
- Project summaries and technical documentation
- Published research and papers
- Personal website content

**If it's not in the knowledge base, the twin won't fabricate it.**

### "I Don't Know" is Good

When the twin says "I don't know" or "I don't have that information," it means:
- The question requires information not in the knowledge base
- The topic needs real-time data or external sources
- The question is outside Barbara's documented expertise

This is a feature, not a bug — accuracy over speculation.

### Project Walkthroughs

For featured projects, you can trigger an interactive walkthrough:
- Ask to "walk through" a specific project
- The twin will guide you through architecture, tech stack, and key decisions
- Includes SVG diagrams when available
- Use "back", "next", or "menu" to navigate
